JUSTIFIED ART! Submission Form

Thank you for submitting to Justified Art! Please fill out the following form and upload images of your work. Make sure to complete the required fields marked by an asterisk (*). Before submitting your work, please review the submission guidelines (found here), the image guidelines (here), as well as all of the information provided on our homepage. We are unable to consider any work if the accompanying electronic form is not completed and submitted by the deadline.

Artist Information







Upload Your Work

You may submit up to three works of art, and for each work you may submit up to three images. Please note that a brief description of each artwork is required in order to be considered for selection by the jury. All images must be in JPEG (.jpg) format only. In the event that the proposed work has not yet been produced, please provide a detailed description of the intent with supporting images of past work. Check to make sure that your images are formatted and named correctly before filling out this form by reading the instructions under Image Formatting Specifications below.
